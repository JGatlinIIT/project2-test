MORTAL KOMBAT

About

Mortal Kombat is one of the most popular video games to ever exist. With its first game releasing back in 1992. with it being the first of its kind, it made huge marks as the first truly gruesome game ever created.

Kharacters

Throughout Mortal Kombat history there have been two characters that are the most well-known for the franchise. those characters are Sub-Zero and Scorpion. These two have been featured on many covers of Mortal Kombat various games. typically within the story they are enemies of each other yet in recent games they have become allies. Along with being the faces of many games they were also the face of many Mortal Kombat movies.

Story

Throughout its time Mortal Kombat has had many different stories both featured in movies and games. one of the most well-known stories follow Mortal Kombat 9(2011). In the story they follow the Mortal Kombat tournament Hosted by the villain Shao Kahn. within this story Raiden( Defender of Earthrealm) has prepared his champion Liu Kang( Earthrealm's champion). The story ends with Earth's realm defeating Shao Kahn.
